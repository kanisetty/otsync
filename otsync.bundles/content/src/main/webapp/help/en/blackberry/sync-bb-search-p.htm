To search your Tempo Box folder:

  1. Click .

  2. Type your search term.

  3. Press the BlackBerry Enter key. Your search results display.

  4. Highlight the file or folder that you want to view.

  5. Press the BlackBerry Menu key to display the available options.

To open the location of an item from your search results:

  1. Search your Tempo Box folder.

  2. Highlight the file that you want to open at the file location.

  3. Press the BlackBerry Menu key and select Open Location.
