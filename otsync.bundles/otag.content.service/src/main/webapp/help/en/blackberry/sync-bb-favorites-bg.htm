To add a favorite:

  1. Click and navigate to the target file.

  2. Highlight the file.

  3. Press the BlackBerry Menu key and select Add to Favorites.

    The file is marked with the overlay. For example, .

  4. Optional If the overlay appears, the file is not downloaded. For example, . To download the file, open the file or sync your favorites. For details, see To Manually Sync Favorites below.

    To sync your favorite files automatically, see Changing OpenText Tempo Box Settings.

    A record of your favorite files is stored on your profile. Profiles are specific to your device. You must create a profile and tag files as favorites on each of your smartphones and tablets running the OpenText Tempo Box app. For more information, see Creating a Profile.

To view a favorite:

  1. Click .

  2. Optional To ensure you are viewing all of your favorites, you should periodically refresh the display. Press the BlackBerry Menu key and select Refresh. This does not download the files for offline viewing. To download a file, sync your favorites or open the file. For details, see To Manually Sync Favorites below.

To open the location of a favorite file:

  1. Click .

  2. Highlight the file that you want to open at the file location.

  3. Press the BlackBerry Menu key and select Open Location.

To remove a favorite:

  1. Click , or click and navigate to the target file.

  2. Highlight the file you want to remove from your favorites list.

  3. Press the BlackBerry Menu key and select Remove from Favorites. The file no longer appears in your list of favorites. This does not delete the file from your Tempo Box folder.

To manually sync favorites:

  1. Click .

  2. Press the BlackBerry Menu key and select Sync Favorites.

    If the app is in the process of synchronizing your favorites or if you are offline, the Sync Favorites option is not available.

     

    Note:
    Selecting Refresh updates the display of the screen. It does not download your favorite files to your BlackBerry for offline viewing. To download the file, you must sync your favorite files or open the file.

     

To sync your favorite files automatically, see Changing OpenText Tempo Box Settings.
