To move or copy a folder:

  1. Click and navigate to the target folder.

  2. Highlight the folder that you want to move or copy.

    You can move shared folders that you own to any location in your Tempo Box folder, except to another shared folder.

    If you copy a folder that is shared with you, a private copy of the folder is created at the new location, but the original shared folder remains at the root level of your Tempo Box folder. You cannot move a folder that is shared with you.
