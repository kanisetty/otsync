Viewing, Editing, and Working With Your Assignments

 

To view your assignments:

  1. Tap Assignments and then tap for the workflow you want to view. Workflow assignments viewed in the ECM Everywhere app do not support HTML tags in the instructions or comments regions. If the instructions do not render properly, remove the HTML tags from the Workflow Map in Content Server.

  2. Edit the attributes, if applicable.

  3. Tap Send on.

To edit attributes:

  1. Tap Assignments and then tap for the workflow you want to view.

  2. Tap the attribute row, and then type the required information or tap and select from the list of options provided.

    Required attributes are marked with an asterisk.

  3. Tap Send on.

For information on working with your assignments, refer to the other sections in Viewing, Editing, and Working With Your Assignments.
