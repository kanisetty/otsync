Adding, Viewing, Removing, and Syncing Favorites

To add a favorite file or folder:

  1. Tap Enterprise Workspace or Personal Workspace and navigate to the target file or folder.

  2. Touch and hold the file or folder that you want to tag as a favorite.

  3. Tap Add to Favorites.

    The file or folder is marked with the overlay. For example, . This gives you easy access to the file or folder from the Favorites app. For folders, this creates a shortcut to the folder. It does not favorite the files and subfolders in the folder.

  4. Optional If the overlay appears, the file is not downloaded. For example, . To download the file, sync your favorites or open the file. For details, see Manually Syncing Your Favorites.

    To sync your favorite files automatically, see Changing OpenText ECM Everywhere Settings.

    A record of your favorite files is stored on your profile. Profiles are specific to your device. You must create a profile and tag files as favorites on each of your smartphones and tablets that run the OpenText ECM Everywhere app.

To view a favorite:

  1. Tap Favorites.

  2. Optional To ensure you are viewing the most up-to-date content, you should periodically refresh the display. For details, see Refreshing the Display.

    This does not download the files for offline viewing. To download the file, sync your favorites or open the file. For details, see Manually Syncing Your Favorites below.

To open the location of a favorite file:

  1. Tap Favorites.

  2. Touch and hold the file for which you want to open the file location.

  3. Tap Open Location.

To remove a favorite:

  1. Tap Favorites, or tap Enterprise Workspace or Personal Workspace and navigate to the target file.

  2. Touch and hold the file you want to remove from your favorites list.

  3. Tap Remove from Favorites. The file no longer appears in your list of favorites. This does not delete the file from your workspace.

To manually sync favorites:

  1. Tap Favorites.

  2. Tap Sync. If the app is in the process of synchronizing your favorites or if you are offline, Sync is not available.

     

    Note:
    Tapping Refresh updates the display of the Favorites page. It does not download your favorite files to your device for offline viewing. To download the file, you must sync your favorite files or open the file.

     

    To sync your favorite files automatically, see Changing OpenText ECM Everywhere Settings.
