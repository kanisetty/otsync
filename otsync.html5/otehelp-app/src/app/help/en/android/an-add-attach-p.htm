Adding an Attachment

To add an attachment:

  • When adding a status update or commenting on a post, tap Attach and then do one of the following:

    • Tap From Camera to attach a picture using the Android camera. Take the picture and then follow the on-screen instructions to save the picture.

    • Tap From File System to attach a file that is stored on your Android device. Navigate to the file and then tap it.

    • Tap From Gallery to attach a picture from the Android photo gallery. Navigate to the picture and then tap it.
