Adding Comments

 

To comment on a post in Feeds:

  1. Tap Feeds.

  2. Tap the post you want to comment on.

  3. Tap Reply.

  4. Type your comment in the text box.

  5. Optional To bring your post to the attention of a specific colleague, use an @ mention. For more information on mentions, see Mentioning a User and Viewing Mentions.

    To add an attachment, see Adding an Attachment.

  6. Tap Post.

 

Tip:
To comment on a post at the item location, see Commenting on a File or Folder.
