Mentioning a User and Viewing Mentions

To mention a user:

  • When adding a status update or commenting on a post, type @<username> with no space in between. This adds the comment to the list of Pulse Mentions in your profile.

To view the posts in which you were mentioned:

  1. Tap Feeds.

  2. Tap Filter and then tap Pulse Mentions.

  3. To return to a regular feed, tap Filter and then tap Pulse Statuses, Pulse Content, Pulse Users I Follow or All.
