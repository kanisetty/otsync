Liking a Post

 

To like a post in Feeds:

  1. Tap Feeds.

  2. Tap a post.

  3. Tap Like. To remove the action, click Remove.

  4. Tap the Android Back key to return to the Feeds app.

To like a post at the item location:

  1. In Enterprise Workspace or Personal Workspace, navigate to a target item.

  2. Touch and hold the item and then tap Comments.

  3. Tap a post and then tap Like.

  4. Tap the Android Back key to return to the workspace.
