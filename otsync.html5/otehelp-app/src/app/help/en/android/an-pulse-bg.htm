Filtering, Viewing, Commenting On, and Searching Posts in Feeds

To filter Feeds:

  1. Tap Feeds.

  2. Tap Filter and then tap one of the following:

    • Pulse Statuses – displays status posts of everyone.

    • Pulse Content – displays content posts of everyone.

    • Pulse Mentions – displays all posts in which you are mentioned.

    • Pulse Users I Follow – displays all posts of only the users whom you are following.

    • All – displays the status and content posts of all Content Server users.

For information on viewing, commenting on, and searching posts, refer to the other sections in Filtering, Viewing, Commenting On, and Searching Posts in Feeds.
