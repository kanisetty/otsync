Searching Feeds

To search feeds:

  1. Tap Feeds. Searching is performed on the active feed. To determine the active feed, look at the row directly below the Search, Filter, and Status buttons.

  2. Optional To change the feed on which you want to search, tap Filter and select one of the filter options. For details, see Filtering, Viewing, Commenting On, and Searching Posts in Feeds.

  3. Tap the search box and type a search string. The search is performed on all of the updates posted. You cannot search for users or attachments.

  4. Tap Search.
