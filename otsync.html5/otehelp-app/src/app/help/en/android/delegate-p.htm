Delegating a Step

To delegate a step:

  1. Tap Assignments.

  2. Tap a workflow task in your list of assignments.

  3. Tap Delegate.

  4. Tap the Assignee field.

    The Assignee field is required. The Delegate button is unavailable until this field is populated.
