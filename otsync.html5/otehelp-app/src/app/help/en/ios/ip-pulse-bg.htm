Filtering, Viewing, Commenting On, and Searching Updates in Feeds

 

To filter Feeds:

  1. Tap Feeds.

  2. Tap Filter and then tap one of the following:

    • Pulse Statuses – displays status updates of everyone.

    • Pulse Content – displays content updates of everyone.

    • Pulse Mentions – displays all updates in which you are mentioned.

    • Pulse Users I Follow – displays all updates of only the users whom you are following.

    • All – displays the status and content updates of all Content Server users.

For information on viewing, commenting on, and searching posts in Feeds, refer to the other topics in Filtering, Viewing, Commenting On, and Searching Posts in Feeds.
