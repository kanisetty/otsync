Enabling Notifications

 

To enable notifications:

  1. Tap Settings for your mobile device.

  2. Tap Notification Center.

  3. Tap Everywhere 10.

  4. Enable the type of notification you want to receive.

  5. Tap the ON switch for Show in Notification Center.

 

Note:
Notifications for Feeds in the OpenText ECM Everywhere app are distinct from the notification settings for Pulse in Content Server, therefore you must configure notifications on each system separately.
