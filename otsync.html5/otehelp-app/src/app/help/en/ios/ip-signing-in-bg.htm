Editing and Managing Your Profile

To edit your profile:

  1. On the Profile screen, touch and hold your profile and then tap Edit.

     

    Tip:
    If you are signed in, tap to return to the Landing screen and then tap Settings > Logout to return to the Profile screen.

     

  2. Change the user name or server information as required.

  3. Tap Done.

To edit your password:

  1. On the Profile screen, tap your profile.

     

    Tip:
    If you are signed in, tap to return to the Landing screen and then tap Settings > Logout to return to the Profile screen.

     

  2. Tap the Password box and type your new password.

  3. Tap Done.

For information on managing your profile, refer to the other topics in Editing and Managing Your Profile.
