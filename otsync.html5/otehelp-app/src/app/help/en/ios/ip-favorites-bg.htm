Viewing Files When Offline

To view files while offline:

  1. You need to open the files and tag them as favorites when you are online. For details, see Adding, Viewing, Removing, and Syncing Favorites.

    Files tagged as favorites are indicated by the overlay. For example, .

    If the overlay appears, the favorite file is not downloaded. For example, . Therefore, the file will not be available for viewing when you go offline. To download the file, open the file or sync your favorites when you are online. For details, see Adding, Viewing, Removing, and Syncing Favorites.

  2. Tap Favorites and tap the file you want to view.

    The files you open when you are offline may not be the latest versions because any edits made to files using a client that is online cannot sync to your device.
