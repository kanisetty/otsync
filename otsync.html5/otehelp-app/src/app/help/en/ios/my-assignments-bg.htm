Viewing, Editing, and Working With Your Assignments

To view your assignments:

  1. Tap Assignments and then tap for the workflow you want to view. Workflow assignments viewed in OpenText ECM Everywhere do not support HTML tags in the instructions or comments regions. If the instructions do not render properly, remove the HTML tags from the Workflow Map in Content Server.

  2. Edit the attributes.

  3. Tap Send on.

To edit attributes:

  1. Tap Assignments and then tap for the workflow you want to view.

  2. Tap the attribute row, and then type the required information or tap and select from the list of options provided.

    Required attributes are marked with an asterisk.

  3. Tap Send on.

To add a colleague in the User Field attribute:

  1. In Assignments, tap a workflow task in your list of assignments.

  2. Tap the user field attribute.

  3. Tap the text box.

  4. Type the name of your colleague.

  5. Tap to select your colleague in the list of results.
