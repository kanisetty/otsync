Adding a Picture

 

To add a picture:

  1. When adding a status update or commenting on a post, tap Attach and then do one of the following:

    • Tap From Camera to attach a picture using the Apple camera.

      Take the picture and then tap Use Photo to use the current picture, or tap Retake and take the picture again.

    • Tap From Gallery to attach a picture from the Apple photo album.

      Navigate to the picture that you want to attach and then tap it.

    If you change your mind, you can tap Remove to remove the attachment.

  2. Type your comment in the text box and tap Post.
