Changing Your Profile Picture

 

To change your profile picture:

  1. On the Landing screen, tap Settings.

  2. Tap Click to change photo.

  3. Do one of the following:

    • Tap From Camera to set your profile picture using the Apple camera.

      Take the picture and then tap Use Photo to use the current picture, or tap Retake and take the picture again.

    • Tap From Album to set your profile picture using a photo from the Apple photo album.

      Navigate to the picture that you want to use and then tap the picture.

    • Tap Set To Default to set your profile picture using the default profile picture.

  4. Tap Settings.
