Removing a Collaborator

To remove a collaborator:

  1. Tap Tempo Box and navigate to the target shared folder.

  2. Tap for the folder for which you want to remove a collaborator.

  3. Swipe your finger horizontally over the name of the person with whom you want to stop sharing the folder. A Remove button appears in the folder row.

    If nothing happens when you swipe your finger, you are not the owner of this folder. Only the folder owner, which is indicated in the Sharing with region, can remove collaborators.

  4. Tap Remove. This will sever the connection to the shared folder and delete the shared folder from your collaboratorâ€™s Tempo Box folder.

  5. Tap Back to return to your files.
