To sign in:

  1. To access your Tempo Box folder, you must first create a profile before you can sign in. For details, see Creating a Profile.

  2. Highlight your profile and press the BlackBerry Trackpad key.

  3. If prompted, type your password. Your password is case-sensitive. Ensure you are typing the password correctly.

    If you are outside of the company, type the password you chose when you created your OpenText Tempo Box account. If you canâ€™t remember your password, refer to the email that was sent to you when you created your OpenText Tempo Box account.

  4. Optional Select the Keep me logged in check box to allow for immediate access the next time you launch the app. The only exceptions are if you log out of the app or if the session times out. See the Caution below that relates to this option.

     

    Note:
    This check box is not available if your BlackBerry is offline.

     

  5. Press the BlackBerry Enter key.

 

Caution
Allowing OpenText Tempo Box to keep you logged in can compromise security. If your BlackBerry device is used by another user when you have not logged out of the OpenText Tempo Box app, the other user will be able to access your OpenText Tempo Box account. To log out, press the BlackBerry Menu key and select Log Out.

 

For information on managing your profile, refer to Signing In and Managing Your Profile.
