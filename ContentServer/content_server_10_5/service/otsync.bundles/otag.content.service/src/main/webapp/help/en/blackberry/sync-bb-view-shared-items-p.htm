To view files in shared folders:

  1. Click . If you have share invitations, a list of share invitations displays. Before you can view the content of a shared folder, the folder owner must share the folder with you. This sends you a share invitation that you must accept. For details on sharing a folder, see Sharing a Folder and Managing Shared Folders.

     

    Tip:
    You have a share invitation when an overlay appears. For example, .

     

  2. Optional To ensure you are viewing all of your share invitations, you should periodically refresh the display. Press the BlackBerry Menu key and select Refresh.

  3. Highlight a share invitation.

  4. Press the BlackBerry Menu key and select Accept.

    The shared folder is placed at the root of your Tempo Box folder. The name of the shared folder is appended with the user name of the folder owner. For example, Marketing Materials (tsmith).

    You will either have read/write or read-only access to the content in the shared folder.

    With read/write access, you can access and edit the content in a shared folder as if it was your own. Any changes made to the content are synced with all other collaborators who have share access to the folder, including the owner. Read/write shared folders are identified by the overlay.
