Changing an External User Password

To change an external user password:

  1. In the top, right pane, hover over your name, and then click Profile.

  2. In the Password section, click the Click here to reset password link to reset your password.

  3. Type your email address and click Save Changes. You should receive an email containing details on how to reset your password.
